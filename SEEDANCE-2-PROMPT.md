# Seedance 2.0 — Hero Video Prompt

## Configuração recomendada

- Modo: Image-to-Video / Reference-to-Video
- Referência: arte oficial da marca ZÊNITE OFERTAS
- Resolução: 1280 × 720 (720p)
- Proporção: 16:9
- Duração: 8 segundos
- Frame rate: 24 fps
- Áudio: desativado / sem som
- Câmera: fixa, sem cortes
- Loop: contínuo; primeiro e último frame devem coincidir

## Prompt principal — copiar e colar

Use the uploaded ZÊNITE OFERTAS logo artwork as the exact visual reference and preserve the brand identity with pixel-level consistency. Keep the original spelling, accent marks, typography, proportions, colors, lightning-bolt symbol, circular composition, spacing, and centered alignment unchanged throughout the entire shot.

Create an 8-second premium cinematic motion-graphics video designed specifically as a website hero background. The scene begins with the complete ZÊNITE OFERTAS logo centered inside a deep navy-blue circular emblem against a dark, elegant blue-black background. Use a locked camera, perfectly stable framing, 16:9 composition, and generous safe margins around the logo so it remains fully visible on desktop, tablet, and mobile crops.

Animate only refined brand-supporting elements: a thin electric-cyan rim light travels smoothly around the edge of the circular emblem; subtle blue energy pulses move behind the logo; a few small luminous particles drift slowly in the background; controlled light reflections pass gently across the emblem; and a restrained cyan glow breathes softly without changing the logo itself. Motion must feel premium, minimal, realistic, smooth, and suitable for a high-end digital-offers brand website.

The logo must remain sharp, readable, stable, and undistorted at all times. Do not morph, redraw, rotate, stretch, translate, blur, or replace any letter or symbol. Do not add new words, icons, products, people, interfaces, borders, watermarks, subtitles, or extra logos. Avoid aggressive flashes and avoid excessive particles.

SEAMLESS LOOP REQUIREMENT: the final state must return precisely to the opening state. The first and last frames must have the same logo position, scale, camera framing, background lighting, cyan glow intensity, rim-light position, and particle arrangement. Use a smooth cyclic animation with no fade to black, no visible cut, no brightness jump, and no pause at the loop point. The transition from the final frame back to the first frame must be visually imperceptible during automatic website playback.

Technical output: 1280x720, 720p, 16:9, 24 fps, high temporal consistency, clean edges, low compression artifacts, no audio, no music, no voice, no captions.
